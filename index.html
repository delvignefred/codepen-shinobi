Shinobi忍者

Profil

A shadow spy and unparalleled warrior, he can work alone or as part of a team.

Depending on the clan's needs, he serves as an informant or can be deployed as an assassin.

Skills

A master of disguise and concealment, he can seamlessly blend into the population to gather the information needed to complete his mission.

He also excels in ancestral martial arts, whose secrets he and his clan carefully preserve.

Spells

Drawing inspiration from the esoteric doctrines of the Yamabushi, he practices occult techniques to gain psychic and supernatural powers.
